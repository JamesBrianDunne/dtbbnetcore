Who are we?

Downtown Big Band is an incredible 21 piece Big Band made up of some of the most talented musicians in Ireland.

Founded in 2015, our mission is to bring Big Band Music to the masses. To date, we have had sell out performances of which we have raised considerable amounts for Irish Charities.

Featuring music from famous artists including Michael Buble, Frank Sinatra, Buddy Rich, Herbie Hancock, Average White Band, Robbie Williams to name just a few, we guarantee to have you up out of your seat!

Led by our Director Mr Paul Seeber and Musical Director Mr Brendan Breslin, this is one Big Band that you don't want to miss!

For Private Bookings/Weddings, please contact us and we will be delighted to discuss future opportunities.
